# Assignmentmobileappadamakib Vehicle Loan Calculator #

A simple Android application to calculate your vehicle loan details.

## Features

*   Calculate total loan amount.
*   Calculate total interest payable.
*   Calculate total payment over the loan period.
*   Calculate your monthly installment.

## How to Use

1.  Enter the vehicle price.
2.  Enter the down payment amount.
3.  Enter the loan period in years.
4.  Enter the annual interest rate.
5.  Click "Calculate" to see the results.
